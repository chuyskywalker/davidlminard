About David

Biography

A multi-talented bay area native, David has been recording sound since 2010 for both video productions and music in studio. While possesing a Bachelor's degree in Recording Engineering from Ex'pression College, David is also a musician, himself (drums, singing, beatboxing).

David has produced content for companies such as The Gap, Google, AOL, Funny or Die, and Whole Foods Market and spent 5 years as the lead recording engineer at Live Musicians Co-op's "4 Legends Studios."

David wears a second hat as a portrait and event photographer and has been shooting for over three years.
